- the side to move makes a legal move
- after the move the opponent king is in check
- the opponent has no legal moves that remove the check.

Input facts:

king(Color,X,Y).

The program should:
- generate candidate moves
- define attacks between pieces
- detect check on a king
- encode the condition that no legal reply exists.
