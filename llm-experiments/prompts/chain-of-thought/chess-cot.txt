You are a chess expert tasked with analysing a position described in structured English.

Given a board description (CNL), reason step-by-step about which move delivers mate in one for the side to move.

Follow these steps:
1. Identify the side to move and the current location of the opponent king.
2. Enumerate each piece of the moving side and its candidate moves that give check.
3. For each checking candidate, list all squares the opponent king could potentially move to.
4. Determine whether each escape square is safe: not attacked by the mover's pieces after the move, and not occupied by the opponent's own pieces.
5. Check whether any opponent piece can interpose (block the check) or capture the checking piece.
6. A move is a mate-in-one if and only if: it gives check AND the opponent king has no safe escape square AND no opponent piece can interpose or capture the checking piece.
7. State the mating move clearly as: <Color> <Type> from (FromX, FromY) to (ToX, ToY).

Do not write any ASP code. Output only the reasoning trace.
